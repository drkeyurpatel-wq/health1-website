Our Story

Built in 2017. Built to Keep Growing.

Health1 Super Speciality Hospitals began as a single facility in Shilaj in 2017. Today, the network spans six hospitals across Gujarat and Rajasthan, with NABH accreditation, dual robotic surgery systems, and a growing organ transplant program.

Accreditations & Quality

Health1 Shilaj is NABH-accredited, reflecting nationally benchmarked standards for patient safety and clinical quality. Nearly 4,000 patients have rated their care 5 out of 5 on Google, independently verified by RepOcean.

Governance

Health1 is governed by a board with equal family ownership across its founding directors. The group's expansion is funded through a combination of operating cash flow and structured debt instruments available to eligible investors. For governance or partnership inquiries, contact our corporate office directly.

Press & Community

Health1 has hosted major medical education events including RoboLapCon, a robotic and laparoscopic surgery conference drawing delegates from across India. For media inquiries, reach our corporate office.
