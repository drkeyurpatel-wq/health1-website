What Actually Matters When Choosing

Fellowship training and volume

Joint replacement outcomes are strongly linked to how many the surgeon performs and their specific arthroplasty training. A high-volume, fellowship-trained surgeon is the foundation of a good result.

Technology that adds precision

Robotic assistance improves implant alignment accuracy, one factor in a well-functioning, long-lasting joint. But the technology supports the surgeon — it does not replace skill.

Accredited hospital and rehabilitation

A good result needs more than surgery — infection control, accredited facilities and structured physiotherapy afterwards are what turn a good operation into an excellent recovery.

Common Questions

Who is the best orthopedic surgeon in Ahmedabad for knee replacement?

The best choice is a fellowship-trained, high-volume arthroplasty surgeon in an accredited hospital with good physiotherapy support. Health1’s team includes an Oxford-certified joint replacement surgeon and uses the Cuvis robotic system. Call 079 6999 6999 to book an assessment.

Is a robotic surgeon better for knee replacement?

Robotic assistance improves implant alignment accuracy, often meaning a smoother recovery — but the surgeon’s skill and experience remain central. The ideal is an experienced arthroplasty surgeon using robotic technology.

What should I ask an orthopaedic surgeon before knee replacement?

Ask about their arthroplasty training and how many they perform, the hospital’s infection rates and accreditation, the implant recommended and why, and how rehabilitation is structured. Honest answers indicate a surgeon worth trusting.
