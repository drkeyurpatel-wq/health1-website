About Dr. Kiran Prajapati

Dr. Kiran Prajapati is a specialist in Interventional Cardiology at Health1 — Shilaj, focusing on catheter-based treatment of blocked heart arteries.

Interventional cardiology treats blocked heart arteries through a small catheter — angiography to find blockages and angioplasty with a stent to open them — without open-heart surgery. Health1's Shilaj Cathlab operates 24×7.
