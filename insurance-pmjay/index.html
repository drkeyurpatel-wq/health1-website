How Cashless Works at Health1

Share your policy or PMJAY card

At admission (or ideally before, for planned procedures), share your insurance policy details or PMJAY card with our billing desk.

We handle pre-authorization

Our team submits the pre-authorization request to your insurer or TPA on your behalf.

Treatment proceeds

Once approved, your treatment proceeds — paperwork is handled by the hospital, not by you mid-treatment.

Discharge

For a fully cashless case, you settle only the non-covered portion (if any) at discharge.

Note: Not all schemes are processed at every branch — confirm with your nearest Health1 branch before admission to avoid surprises.

Common Questions

Is PMJAY accepted at all branches?

PMJAY is accepted across Health1 branches, though specific procedure coverage can vary by location — confirm with your nearest branch before admission.

What if my insurer isn't fully covering the treatment?

You'll be informed of any non-covered portion before treatment where possible, and settle only that amount at discharge for a cashless case.

Can I switch from cashless to reimbursement if something isn't approved?

Yes — our billing team can guide you through submitting for reimbursement directly with your insurer if pre-authorization isn't approved in time.
