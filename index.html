Common Questions

Before You Ask

Is PMJAY accepted, and at which branches?

Yes — PMJAY is accepted across Health1 branches. Coverage for specific procedures can vary by location, so confirm with your nearest branch before admission.

Which insurance companies does Health1 work with for cashless treatment?

Health1 is empanelled with virtually every major insurer and TPA in India, along with government schemes like CGHS, ECHS, and corporate tie-ups including GEB, ONGC, and Railways.

What happens in a medical emergency?

Every Health1 branch runs a 24×7 emergency department with an on-call intensive care team. Call the branch emergency number directly — it's faster than any online form.

How is robotic knee replacement different from conventional surgery?

The Cuvis robotic system places implants with sub-millimeter precision. Most patients begin walking with support the same day, versus several days for conventional surgery.

Can I get a second opinion before committing to surgery?

Yes — for complex or life-changing decisions like transplant or major surgery, you can request a second opinion from a senior Health1 specialist before proceeding.

What's the process for international or NRI patients?

Health1 assists with advance cost estimates and visa-related documentation for international patients. Contact the corporate office to start the process before you travel.
